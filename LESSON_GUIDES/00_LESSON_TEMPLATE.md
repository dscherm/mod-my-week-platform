# Quick Reference: 5E Model

| Phase | Time | Student Role | Teacher Role | Platform Use |
|-------|------|--------------|--------------|--------------|
| **Explore** | 10-15 min | Discover, question | Observe, probe | Optional demo |
| **Explain** | 15-20 min | Listen, discuss | Direct teach | Reference |
| **Apply** | 15-20 min | Guided practice | Coach, support | Challenges |
| **Practice** | 15-20 min | Independent work | Monitor, assist | Deep use |
| **Challenge** | 10-15 min | Demonstrate mastery | Assess | CTF/Assessment |
